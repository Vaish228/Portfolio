SMART WATER TAX CALCULATION METER

A smart water tax calculation meter can be built using Arduino IoT, a water valve, and some jump wires. The Arduino monitors water usage through sensors and controls the valve to manage flow.

It calculates the water tax based on usage data, sending real-time information to a connected device. This system ensures accurate billing and efficient water management. Simple components and coding make it a cost-effective solution.
